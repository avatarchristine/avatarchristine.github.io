The Organization

The University of the Philippines Association of Computer Science Majors (UP CURSOR) is a nonprofit, non-stock, nonsectarian socio-civic-academic student organization in the College of Engineering, UP Diliman, Quezon City. It was formed by an informal group of 15 students majoring in Computer Science back in August 1983 but was recognized only a year later. It was argued that a Computer Science organization is not needed anymore since there exists a UP Computer Society; however, having the latter university-wide and university-based then paved the way for the recognition of a college-based Computer Science organization.
